Kuk Sool Won of Linlithgow

Kuk Sool Won is a traditional Korean martial art. We offer classes for all ages in Linlithgow, focusing on self-defense, fitness, and discipline.

Kicking Koalas

For kids under 8, our Kicking Koalas are a great way for kids to learn and grow! Kuk Sool Won teaches children teamwork, discipline, and respect in a fun-filled environment. We focus on building the physical and mental foundations needed for success both in and out of the dojang.

Kids

Kuk Sool Won is an exceptional discipline for children, focusing on more than just physical movement. Our structured classes help kids develop crucial life skills, including respect, self-control, and concentration. The training significantly improves coordination, balance, and fitness in a safe and supportive environment.

Youths & Adults

Kuk Sool Won offers adults a comprehensive path to physical and mental fitness. It integrates striking, joint locks, throws, and weapon training, making it highly effective for self-defense. Our classes foster better focus, stress relief, and a deep sense of accomplishment as you master complex techniques.
